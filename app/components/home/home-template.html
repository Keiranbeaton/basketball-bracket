Scoring Info

You get 1 point for each winner you get right in the first round, 2 for each right answer in the conference semis, 4 for the conference finals and 8 for picking the champs correctly. If you pick the winner for a series, you get 1 bonus point for picking the series length regardless of what round that series is in.
